HISTORY

The Detroit Red Wings were originally known as the Victoria Cougars in the 1920's until their roster was sold to a group from Detroit on September 25, 1926. The team played as the Detroit Cougars in 1926 and its home games were played in Windsor, Ontario. The Team then moved into the new Olympia Stadium in the 1927-28 season. The Olympia Stadium was home of the franchise through the 1979-80 season. Today the team plays at Joe Louis Arena.
